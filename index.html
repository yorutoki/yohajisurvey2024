Top 1 favorite & 3 other favorites

Once again, Haruaki sweeps, getting 37% of the votes!

Dr. Takahashi moves up to 2nd place from only getting 1 out of 22 votes last time! Being a manwhore truly does wonders 😌

Rintarou, while losing to Takahashi as a "no. 1" pick, beats him and nearly ties with Haruaki as a "other 3 faves" pick!

And rounding out 4th and 5th place are the Principal and Sano, tied for votes.
Mame and Hatanaka, while not many people's first choice, are in a lot of people's "top 4" picks! Quite in-character for them.

Kai and Seiryuu are tied for first among the characters who weren't anyone's "no. 1", but were in their "other 3 faves".

Nyuudou, who got 2nd place with 3 votes in the 2022 poll, got the same amount of votes this time, but unfortunately that only puts him at tied for 11th.
